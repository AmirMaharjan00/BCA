About Shahid Smarak College

Shahid Smarak College, the first Community college of Kirtipur, has been imparting quality education to the students of more than 68 districts of Nepal for 25 long years in reasonable fees. It is named to pay tribute and immortalize the contribution of the martyrs who sacrificed their precious lives for a full-fledged democracy in nepal in 2046 BS. The college has taken a giant leap since it initiated NEB. affiliated + 2 programes in Management, Humanities and Education streams. On the way to extend its academic arena, the college has become successful in introducing social work in BA form the academic session 2070/71 BS.

Shahid Smarak (SS) College owes its outstanding performance and nation-wide growing popularity due to the apt vision of the management committee, unshakable faith of its guardians and laborious students. Above all , the devotion and dedication of the faculty members, who possess cutting edge knowledge and diligence, industrious non-teaching staff have given the college an accelerating boost up in marching ahead on the path of academic excellence.

Mission :

The mission of SS college is to develop and train the young talents of Nepal by cultivating a high blend of knowledge, advanced academic skills and practical orientation for preparing them to pursue further education with full confidence and salable in the global market.

Objectives :

  1. To impart cutting edge knowledge, skill and practical wisdom in students.
  2. To equip the students with full-fledged and balanced, market- friendly curricula that meet the requirements of national and international satandard.
  3. To explore and foster innate potentialities in every individual so as to develop them as vital role players in the holistic development of the nation.
  4. To cultivate the time-tested norms and values in the students to sensitize them to humanity, brotherhood and natural environment.
  5. To make the the higher education accessible to unprivileged, castes, classes and ethnic groups through various scholarship programmes.
  6. To develop the students role model for social and economic transformation.
